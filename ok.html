WALTON PLC

With over 30,000 employees, Walton is one of the largest employers in Bangladesh.
It operates more than 1,000 retail stores under the brand name "Walton Plaza" and has an
international presence in China, India, and the United States.[3][4]
The company has been recognized as a prominent player in the
